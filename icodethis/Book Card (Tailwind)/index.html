Convenience Store Woman

Author: Suyaki Murata

"Convenience Store Woman" explores themes of societal conformity, individuality, and the struggle to find one's place in the world. It also touches on the tension between personal desires and societal expectations, inviting readers to reflect on the complexities of identity and the different paths people take to navigate their lives.
